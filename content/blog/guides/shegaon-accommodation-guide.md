# Shegaon Accommodation Guide

If you are planning a stay in Shegaon, this accommodation guide helps you choose the most suitable Sansthan facility based on family size, convenience, and proximity.

## 1. Shegaon Bhakt Niwas

[Shegaon Bhakt Niwas](/locations/shegaon-bhakt-niwas) is the most common choice for devotees visiting the main temple area. It is practical for families who want to stay close to core darshan routes.

## 2. Shegaon Anand Vihar

[Shegaon Anand Vihar](/locations/shegaon-anand-vihar) is often preferred by devotees who want a calmer environment near Anand Sagar and comfortable room options.

## 3. Shegaon Visawa

[Shegaon Visawa](/locations/shegaon-visawa) is useful for travelers arriving by rail or looking for quick transit access.

## How to choose the right place

- Prefer Bhakt Niwas for direct temple movement.
- Prefer Anand Vihar for a quieter and premium family stay.
- Prefer Visawa for short or transit-oriented visits.

## Booking tips for devotees

- Start with the [booking page](/booking) and submit clear date and family details.
- Keep one contact number active for follow-up.
- Carry valid identity documents for all guests.

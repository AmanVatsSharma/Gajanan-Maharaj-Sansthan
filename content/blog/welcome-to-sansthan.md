# Welcome to Shri Gajanan Maharaj Sansthan

We are delighted to welcome you to the official digital presence of Shri Gajanan Maharaj Sansthan. This platform is designed to help devotees access reliable information for darshan planning, Sansthan accommodation support, and spiritual updates.

## Our Services

The Sansthan offers practical services for the comfort and convenience of devotees:

*   **Accommodation:** Bhakta Niwas facilities for pilgrims.
*   **Darshan:** Organized queues and facilities for smooth Darshan.
*   **Prasad:** Mahaprasad distribution for all devotees.
*   **Guidance:** Booking and travel support through official communication channels.

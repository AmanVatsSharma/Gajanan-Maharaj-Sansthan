# Welcome to Shri Gajanan Maharaj Sansthan

We are delighted to welcome you to the digital presence of Shri Gajanan Maharaj Sansthan. This platform is designed to help devotees connect with the services and spiritual activities of the Sansthan.

## Our Services

The Sansthan offers various facilities for the comfort and convenience of devotees:

*   **Accommodation:** Bhakta Niwas facilities for pilgrims.
*   **Darshan:** Organized queues and facilities for smooth Darshan.
*   **Prasad:** Mahaprasad distribution for all devotees.

## Upcoming Events

Stay tuned for updates regarding:

1.  **Festivals:** Celebrations of major festivals like Ram Navami and Rishi Panchami.
2.  **Palkhi:** Information about Palkhi processions.
3.  **Community Services:** Medical camps and educational initiatives.
